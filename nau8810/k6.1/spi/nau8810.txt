NAU8810 audio CODEC

Required properties:

  - compatible : (required) "nuvoton,nau8810"
  - reg : (required) chip select address of device.
  - spi-max-frequency : (required) Maximum SPI clocking speed of device in Hz

Example:

codec: nau8810@0 {
	compatible = "nuvoton,nau8810";
	spi-max-frequency = <400000>;
	reg = <0>;
};
